Before you start

What you'll need

We'll ask for information about:

  • income - how much you get from any pensions or benefits
  • housing costs - what you pay for things like Council Tax and rent

If you're working

We'll ask you to send us:

  • copies of your payslips to cover the last 5 weeks
  • proof of your Universal Credit application or award notice (if you get Universal Credit)
  • your latest Tax Credits award notice (if you get Tax Credits)

Documents that could help

You may need:

  • rent or mortgage statements
  • latest Council Tax bill or Council Tax benefit letter
  • pension details
  • latest statement of benefits or award notice
  • savings, investments and property details

Warning It's important you know this information before you start.
You can't save your application and come back to it later.
