Experimental

Earnings: Single & Partner Applicants (PAYE, Benefits, Pensions, Maintenance Payments & Nil Income)

Hypothesis

We believe that allowing applicants to submit evidence of their income (PAYE & Benefits) via email and post will be a useful feature for applicants.

What we changed:

  • Changed 'Before Your Start' section to grant access to PAYE, Benefits, Pensions, Maintenance Payments & Nil Income applicants (single & partner applicants).
  • Added in new sections to task list (About your income & About your partners income).
  • Added in PAYE journey for single and partner applicants.
  • Amended Benefits journey to include Universal Credit & Working Tax Credit questions for single and partner applicants.
  • Amended Pensions journey for single and partner applicants.
  • Added Maintenance Payments journey for single and partner applicants.
  • Added in Nil Income user journey for single and partner applicants.
  • If a user enters an email address, we give them the option of whether they would like to submit their evidence by email or post, otherwise we ask them to submit it by post. Available for both single and partner applicants.

What we plan to test:

  • Whether applicants (single & partner) can successfully navigate through the PAYE, Benefits, Pensions, Maintenance Payments & Nil Income workflow and choose how they wish to supply evidence of their income (if required).
  • Whether assessors are satisfied that the following journey gives them all of the required information for single and partner applicants.

What we found:

  • Users who we researched could supply evidence by email if they needed to, but the majority still liked the perceived security of sending in evidence by post.

Known Issues

  • Prototype only includes new / amended screens and is not a true representation of the entire service.
  • No error messages in the prototype.
  • Uses dummy data on final 'check your answer' screens.
