Experimental

Student - Term dates (Re-design),Live at home, Student Loan, Name of course, Advanced students

Hypothesis C

We believe that accepting applications from people who are in full time advanced education and living with parents will be a useful feature . This release also allows students who receive student loans. Students who are in full time education, who live at home, who receives student loan will be allowed to successfully apply digitally for help with health costs. What we changed:

  • Removing 'Are you studying any of these?' screen from 'Before you start'
  • Applicants will be asked the name of place of study, name of course
  • Applicants will be asked term dates (Standard 3 terms dates are requested)
  • Applicants will be asked whether they are an overseas students - this is asked because:

  • On the back of this: we decided to playback the term dates to students to confirm before continuing(next sprint)

Sprint 3 Q4(R5)

Experimental

Student Term Dates & Final year

Hypothesis C

We believe that accepting applications from people who are in full time non advanced education and living with parents / relatives / friends (Not paying / liable for rent). This release also includes students who are not in the final year will be a useful feature as it will allow them to successfully apply digitally for help with health costs. Students who are in full time non-advanced education, who live with parents / relatives / friends will be allowed to successfully apply digitally for help with health costs. This will increase the number of people who can apply digitally by XX%

What we changed:

  • Moving 'Are you in final year or only year of your course' screen from Before you start to 'Education and Training' (Personal Details)
  • Applicants will be asked about their holiday dates (term dates in paper) - this was identified as pain points as applicants unable to find their term dates as some edu establishments uses semester dates instead.

What we plan to test:

  • Whether or not applicants able to find dates for Christmas holidays, Easter holidays and Summer holidays
  • Is asking the term dates by their holiday dates solves the pain point of users finding out their term dates
  • Whether assessors are satisfied that the following journey gives them all of the required information for single applicant.

What we found:

  • Students struggled in a set scenario involving money from a relative at the /money-coming-in page. They felt they needed to say at this point they get money from a relative and thought the closest option was trust fund. Changes made in R6 to include Family or friends in student-funding page.
  • Term dates : Users struggled to identify their holiday dates and some students do not start in Sept - there are Jan / April intake etc - therefore asking for holiday dates will be a blocker for these cluster of users. In addition to that students who are in medical / health field do not have the standard 3 terms (due to having placements etc) and will not be able to continue with the journey. So a decision was made to re-design the user journey for term dates in R6
  • Done Page : User expected to receive Help with Health costs (and thought they can start getting prescription) straight after their application has been submitted by seeing the DONE word in the DONE page. So it was decided to look into the content and potential re-design for this screen in R6




Known Issues

  • No error messages in the prototype.
  • Uses dummy data on final 'check your answer' screens.
