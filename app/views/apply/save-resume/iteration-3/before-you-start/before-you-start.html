Apply online for help with NHS costs

You will now be asked 7 questions to make sure that you are eligible to apply online.

If you are not eligible to apply online, you can apply using the paper form, called a HC1.

How do I get a HC1 form?

You can get a HC1 form from any of the following places:

  • your local pharmacy
  • your GP practice
