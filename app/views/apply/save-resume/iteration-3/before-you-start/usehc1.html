Apply for help with health costs using a HC1 paper form

Based on the responses you have given, you are not currently eligible to apply online. However, you can apply using the paper form, called a HC1.

How do I get a HC1 form?

You can get a HC1 form from any of the following places:

  • your local pharmacy
  • your GP practice

Alternatively, you can:

  • download (opens in a new tab)a printable version of the form.

Once you have completed the paper form, follow the instructions within the form on where to post the application to.
