Before you start

Could you upload your evidence online?

As part of your application, we may ask for evidence of your household income. This could be payslips or benefit award letters.

We will let you know if this is the case and what you need to provide.

We will ask you to upload an image of the document at the end of the application. We will provide instructions on how to do this.

If you do not have digital versions of the document, you can take a photo, scan or screenshot of them. If you're taking a photo, you'll need to use a smartphone or digital camera.

I am employed but do not receive payslips

You can't apply online yet. You need to apply using a paper form called an HC1. Your employer should also complete a proof of income form (LIS82) which can be sent with your application.

Both forms are available to download from the NHS Low Income Scheme website (opens in a new window).

Select no for more details on how to apply.
