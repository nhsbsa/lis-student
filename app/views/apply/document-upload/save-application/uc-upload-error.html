Upload Universal Credit award notice

We need confirmation of your household income to assess your application.

Make sure you upload everything we have asked for to avoid delays to your application. We will email you if we need further information after you have submitted your application.

What you need to upload

Upload a copy of each page of your Universal Credit award notice.

This needs to be the award notice for the last complete assessment period that ended immediately before 22 April 2022.

Sign in to your Universal Credit account to get this from your online statements.

If you do not have an online account, upload the award notice you have been sent.

  • no larger than 10MB
