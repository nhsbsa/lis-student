Upload Payslips

We need confirmation of your household income to assess your application.

Make sure you upload everything you need to, or it could delay your application. We may still need to email you asking for more information after you have uploaded your payslips and submitted your application.

What you need to upload

You need to upload copies of:

  • your last 4 consecutive payslips for your ‘Cleaner’ job, leading up to 22 April
  • your last 2 consecutive payslips for your ‘Shop assistant’ job, leading up to 22 April
  • your last payslip for your ‘Nurse’ job, leading up to 22 April

You can use a digital camera, smartphone, or tablet to take a photo, or use a scanner. If you already have a digital version of your payslips, they will need to be:

  • a PNG, GIF, or PDF file
  • no larger than 2MB
