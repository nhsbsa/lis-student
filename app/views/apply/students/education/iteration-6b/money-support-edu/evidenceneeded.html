What do we need

We need you to send us more information about your Student Finance income.

We need your Student Finance Breakdown from [{{ data['tuitionfee']}}].

We will accept the digital copy and the letter.

How to send your information

You can send your Student Finance Breakdown by either email or post

Send your information by email

  1. 1

    Take a photo of your Student Finance Breakdown using a mobile phone or digital camera.

    It is important that the details are clearly shown in the photo.

  2. 2

    Check the image is clear and you can read the information.

  3. 3

    Send the photo as an email attachment to: nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

    You must include your Application Reference number 01234567890.

    We need to be able to confirm your identity, so your email must also include your:

    • first name and surname (family name)
    • date of birth
    • address including postcode
  4. 4

    Once we receive your email with your proof, we will check it and contact you within 2 working days.

Send your information by post

Take a photocopy of your student finance breakdown and send it to us at:

Low Income Scheme Team

152 Pilgrim Street
Newcastle Upon Tyne
NE1 6SN
