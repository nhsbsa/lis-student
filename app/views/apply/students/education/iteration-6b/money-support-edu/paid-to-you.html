What is the total amount of student finance paid to you for this academic year?

Where to find your total amount of student finance

Your student finance letter

Your total amount of student finance is shown on your student finance entitlement letter.

It's within the blue box saying 'Total student finance we'll pay to you'.

Your student finance online account

Your total amount of student finance is shown in the payments section of your online account.

It's shown under the heading 'Maintenance loan- paid to you'.

The amount must include pence. For example, £8,430.00
