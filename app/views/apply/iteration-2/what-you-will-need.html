Before you start

What you'll need

We'll ask for information about:

  • your household income - how much money comes into your home from wages, pensions or benefits
  • your housing costs – what your household pays for things like Council Tax and rent

If you get them, we might ask you to send us copies of:

  • payslips, if you or your partner have a job
  • your latest Working Tax Credit award notice
  • your Universal Credit award notice

Documents you’ll find helpful

You may need:

  • rent or mortgage statements
  • latest Council Tax bill or Council Tax benefit letter
  • pension details
  • latest statement of benefits or award notice
  • savings, investments and property details

Warning You can’t save your application and come back to it later so you should make sure you have all of the information you need.
