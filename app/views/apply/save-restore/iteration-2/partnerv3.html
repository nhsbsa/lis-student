Before you start

Do you live with a partner?

This means you live together as a couple, you don't have to be married or in a civil partnership.


When can I apply for someone else?

If you're responsible for their financial affairs

You can complete the form on behalf of someone else if you are responsible for their financial affiars because they have either:

  • earning difficulties
  • a condition which prevents them from managing their own affairs

Warning You are responsible for making sure the information entered is correct and complete.

If you're just helping to complete the form

You should select the 'myself' option.

You can help to complete the form, but they are responsible for the information given.
