Wages from a job

Are you paid any work‑related expenses at your '{{ data['jobtitle']}}' job?

A work-related expense is an expense that you are paid back whilst performing the duties of your job.

What is classed as a work‑related expense?

Work-related expenses include:

  • Uniform, work clothing and tool allowance
  • Vehicles you use for work e.g. Company / Hire car
  • Travel and overnight expenses
  • Professional fees and subscriptions
  • Working from home fees
  • Equipment purchase etc...
