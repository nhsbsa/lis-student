Wages from a job

What you'll need

Depending on how often you are paid, you'll need to supply photos or scans of your payslips for each job you have:

  • Paid weekly - last 5 consecutive payslips
  • Paid every 2 weeks - last 4 consecutive payslips
  • Paid every 4 weeks - last 2 consecutive payslips
  • Paid every calendar month - last 2 consecutive payslips
  • Pay varies - every payslip since starting

If you are unable to supply photos or scans of your payslips, you can still submit your application but it will take longer for us to make a decision.

Help with submitting documents

To take a picture of your payslips:

  1. Place your payslip on a flat service in a well lit room. Use a flash if you need to.
  2. Take a good quality picture of the entire payslip. You should be able to see its edges.
  3. Check that the image is in focus. You must be able to read all the writing on the payslip, including the handwriting. Zoom in if you need to check this.
  4. Send the picture to this device (For example, by email) and save it.
