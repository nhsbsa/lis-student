What happens next

You’ll get an email from us within the next 24 hours confirming we’ve received your application.

We’ll assess your application and let you know the outcome by email. Please check your junk and spam folders.

You are not eligible for free NHS prescriptions or treatment until you receive an outcome from us confirming your eligibility. This can take up to 15 working days.

How to apply for a refund

If you pay NHS prescription charges then find out later that you’re entitled to free prescriptions you may be able to claim a refund.

You’ll need to ask your pharmacist for an FP57 refund receipt when you pay as you can’t get one later. Find out more about how to claim a refund.
