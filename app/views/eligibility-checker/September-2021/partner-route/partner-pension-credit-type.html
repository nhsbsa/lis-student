What type of Pension Credit do you or your partner get?

Pension Credit is a benefit with 2 parts - Guarantee Credit and Savings Credit.

I'm not sure what type I get

Check your benefit payment letter

Guarantee Credit
Guarantee Credit tops up your weekly income if it's below £270.30.

Savings Credit
Savings Credit is an extra payment for people who have saved some money towards their retirement, for example a pension.

The most in Savings Credit you can get per week £15.71.
