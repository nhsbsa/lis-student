Although most NHS treatment is free, there is usually a charge for:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing glasses or contact lenses
  • NHS wigs

If your dental practice is in Scotland or Wales

In Scotland, you get free NHS dental check-ups, treatments, and appliances if you're aged 18 to 25.

In Wales, you get free NHS dental check-ups if you're aged 18 to 24 or over 60.
