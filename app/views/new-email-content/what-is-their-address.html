What is Joe's address?

Joe's address will be used for verification of their details. Their help with help costs certificate, if they are eligible, will be sent by email. We do not send certificates in the post for online applications.

If they want a paper certificate, see our website for other ways to apply (opens in a new tab).


I don't know what address to use

If Joe has more than one address

You should use the address that Joe's GP or dentist holds for them. For example, if Joe is a student living away from home, this could be their home or term time address.

If Joe don't have a permanent address

If Joe is homeless and living in temporary accommodation (for example, a long term hostel), you should use the address that their GP or dentist holds for them.
