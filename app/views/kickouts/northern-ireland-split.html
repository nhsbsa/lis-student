If you're pregnant or have children:

As part of the Healthy Start scheme, if you're at least 10 weeks pregnant or have a child under 4 years old, you might be able to get help to buy healthy food and milk.

You would get:

  • £4.25 each week of your pregnancy from the 10th week
  • £8.50 each week for children from birth to 1 year old
  • £4.25 each week for children between 1 and 4 years old

You could also get free vitamin supplements.

Find out more about who qualifies for Healthy Start.
